# AskDoGood Merch Design Specifications
**Version:** April 28, 2026 | **Status:** Ready for Approval

---

## Brand Quick Reference
| Role | Value |
|---|---|
| Deep Forest Green | `#0e3121` |
| Terracotta / Amber | `#cd7c3a` |
| Warm Cream / Off-White | `#f5efe6` |
| Charcoal Black | `#1a1a1a` |
| Sage | `#6b8c7a` |

**Brand voice:** Real. Disciplined. Community-first. Black wellness. Faith-adjacent. Bold but not loud.

**Wordmark rule (all apparel):** Include `AskDoGood.com` in a small sans-serif (8–10pt equivalent) at the back neck OR bottom hem. Terracotta on black garments; forest green on light garments.

## MUGS ($18) — Base: White ceramic or cream matte glaze

---

### 1. The "Gratitude" Mug
**Tagline:** "Less attitude, more gratitude"

**Design Brief**
- Tagline split across two lines: "Less attitude," on line 1, "more gratitude." on line 2.
- Small botanical leaf sprig (single stem, minimalist) tucked beneath the text.
- Optional: small sun icon or dot above the "i" in "gratitude" in terracotta.

**Color Palette**
- Background: Warm cream `#f5efe6` (mug base glaze or wrap print)
- Primary text: Forest green `#0e3121`
- Accent (sprig/dot): Terracotta `#cd7c3a`

**Typography**
- Line 1 "Less attitude," — bold serif (Playfair Display Bold or Cormorant Garamond Bold), slightly smaller
- Line 2 "more gratitude." — same bold serif, slightly larger to give hierarchy
- Optionally hand-lettered script for "more gratitude" to contrast line 1

**Placement**
- Centered on the front face of the mug, vertically centered in the upper half
- Small `AskDoGood.com` in tiny sans-serif at the very bottom of the wrap

**Canva / AI Prompt**
> "Minimalist mug design, warm cream background, deep forest green bold serif text reading 'Less attitude,' on top, 'more gratitude.' below in slightly larger text, small single botanical leaf sprig in terracotta beneath the text, clean and warm wellness brand aesthetic, no clutter"

**Mockup Style**
Flat-lay overhead: mug on a wooden surface with a sprig of fresh herbs or eucalyptus beside it. Morning light feel.

### 2. The "Healing Path" Mug
**Tagline:** "Healing is not linear"

**Design Brief**
- A single wavy, irregular horizontal line drawn across the middle of the design — representing a non-linear path.
- Tagline sits below or overlaps the line.
- Raw, sketch-quality line (not a perfect sine wave — intentionally imperfect).

**Color Palette**
- Background: Warm cream `#f5efe6`
- Text: Charcoal black `#1a1a1a`
- Line accent: Terracotta `#cd7c3a`

**Typography**
- "Healing is not linear" — lowercase, medium-weight sans-serif (e.g., DM Sans Medium or Nunito Regular), centered beneath the line
- Keep it understated — the line does the heavy lifting visually

**Placement**
- Centered on front face; line spans about 60% of design width
- `AskDoGood.com` in tiny text at bottom of wrap

**Canva / AI Prompt**
> "Minimalist mug design, cream background, a single hand-drawn imperfect wavy line in terracotta orange across the center, beneath it the text 'healing is not linear' in small charcoal lowercase sans-serif, quiet emotional wellness aesthetic, no borders or extra elements"

**Mockup Style**
Lifestyle: mug held in two hands, slightly out of focus background showing a window with natural light. Cozy, reflective mood.

- [ ] 6–10: T-shirt designs → export at 300 DPI; left-chest ~3.5"×3.5", full-front ~10"×12"
- [ ] 11–14: Hoodie designs → export at 300 DPI; same size specs as tees + back-neck tag
- [ ] 15–17: Tote designs → export at 300 DPI; ~10"×10" print area (confirm with printer)
- [ ] All apparel → include `AskDoGood.com` back-neck/hem label file (separate art file)
- [ ] All pieces → create Gumroad/Stripe product photo from mockup before listing
- [ ] Confirm print partner (Printful, Printify, or local DMV screen printer) specs before final file export
