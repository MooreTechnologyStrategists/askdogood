# Canva Brief: 21-Day Plant-Based Reset

## Direction
Clean wellness editorial look.

The cover should feel:
- calm
- polished
- trustworthy
- premium without looking clinical
- plant-based without turning into generic stock-food clutter

## Format
- Primary cover: 1600 x 2400
- Thumbnail/social crop: 1280 x 720

## Title Stack
- 21-Day
- Plant-Based
- Reset

## Brand Line
- ASKDOGOOD

## Supporting Line
- Nourishment. Structure. Consistency.

## Main Hook
- Clean up your plate without making life miserable.

## Lower Support Copy
- A practical 21-day framework for anti-inflammatory meal structure, prep rhythm, and stronger routines.

## Badge
- DIGITAL RESET GUIDE

## Visual Language
Use a soft editorial background with:
- warm cream base
- muted sage/green lower landscape shapes
- one gold sun/disc in the top right
- one restrained botanical/produce illustration cluster, not a photo collage

Suggested produce illustration cluster:
- leafy greens
- beet or radish slice
- neutral grain or seed shape

Keep the visuals minimal and airy.
Do not overload the cover with bowls, utensils, smoothies, and groceries all at once.

## Color Palette
- Deep green: #173D2F
- Supporting green: #2A5B46
- Warm cream: #F7F1E7
- Soft sand: #EFE4D4
- Sage: #9ABD93
- Gold accent: #D0AA56
- Body text gray-green: #465347

## Typography
Use a serif for the main title and a clean sans serif for the rest.

Recommended Canva pairings:
- Serif title: Cormorant Garamond, Libre Baskerville, or DM Serif Display
- Sans serif support: Montserrat, Lato, or Source Sans 3

## Layout Rules
- Left-aligned title block
- Large serif title with strong vertical rhythm
- Thin gold divider under title
- Hook directly beneath divider
- One dark pill badge for DIGITAL RESET GUIDE
- Supporting description near bottom third
- Plenty of breathing room

## Avoid
- purple tones
- busy flat-lay food photos
- mock-medical visuals
- too many icons
- gradient-heavy tech styling
- script fonts
- more than one CTA-style element

## Canva Build Order
1. Set cream background.
2. Add soft green landscape shapes at bottom.
3. Add gold sun/disc top right.
4. Add title stack in serif.
5. Add brand line and supporting line.
6. Add gold divider.
7. Add hook in bold sans serif.
8. Add dark green badge with DIGITAL RESET GUIDE.
9. Add one small produce illustration cluster.
10. Add supporting description at bottom.
