### 1. **GREEN GLOW SMOOTHIE** (5 min)
*Energizing breakfast, reduces inflammation*

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups fresh spinach
- 1 frozen banana
- 1 cup frozen mango
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tbsp almond butter
- 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
- 1 tsp fresh ginger (optional)
- Ice cubes

**Instructions:**
1. Add spinach and almond milk to blender first
2. Blend until smooth
3. Add remaining ingredients
4. Blend until creamy (30-60 seconds)
5. Pour into glass, top with hemp seeds

**Nutrition per serving:** 320 calories, 8g protein, 12g healthy fats, 45g carbs, 10g fiber

**Why it works:** High in antioxidants, omega-3s, and anti-inflammatory compounds. Alkalizing greens support detox.

### 5. **THAI RED CURRY** (30 min)
*Restaurant-quality, metabolism-boosting*

**Ingredients:**
- 1 block extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 2 tbsp red curry paste
- 1 can coconut milk (full-fat)
- 1 cup vegetable broth
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 cup snap peas
- 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
- 1 tbsp coconut aminos or tamari
- Juice of 1 lime
- Fresh basil for garnish
- Brown rice for serving

**Instructions:**
1. Cook brown rice according to package
2. Press tofu for 15 min, cube it
3. Heat coconut oil in large pan, cook tofu until golden (8 min), set aside
4. In same pan, add curry paste, cook 1 min
5. Add coconut milk and broth, stir to combine
6. Add vegetables, simmer 10 min
7. Add tofu back in, add coconut aminos and lime
8. Serve over brown rice, garnish with basil

**Nutrition per serving:** 480 calories, 18g protein, 24g fat, 52g carbs, 8g fiber

**Why it works:** Curry spices boost metabolism. Coconut milk provides healthy MCT fats. Tofu offers complete plant protein.
