- Identity-driven
- Practical-results

Use these patterns and swap product-specific language:
- Pain-aware:
  - "Tired of restarting? Use one practical system you can follow."
  - "If low energy keeps derailing your routine, this gives you clear structure."
  - "No more random tips. Get a step-by-step plan."
- Identity-driven:
  - "Become the woman who follows through with structure, not stress."
  - "You do not need perfection. You need consistency."
  - "Choose disciplined healing over short motivation spikes."
- Practical-results:
  - "Clear weekly actions, less guesswork, better follow-through."

## C) 30-Day Rollout + Budget

### Weekly rollout
- Week 1: Launch 21-Day Plant-Based Reset (primary acquisition)
- Week 2: Add Lab Guide retargeting for non-buyers
- Week 3: Launch Thyroid Health Mastery to warm audiences/buyers
- Week 4: Offer Wellness Circle to recent buyers and engaged leads
- Days 29-30: Scale winners, pause underperformers, prep next cycle

### Budget tiers
- Conservative: $15/day
  - $10 Reset prospecting
  - $5 Lab Guide retargeting
- Moderate: $35/day
  - $20 Reset prospecting
  - $8 Lab Guide retargeting
  - $7 Course warm traffic

### First product to test
- Start with 21-Day Plant-Based Reset
- Why: broad relevance, lower friction price, fastest path to conversion data

### KPI targets
- CTR: good above 1.5%, revise below 1.0%
- CVR (landing -> purchase): good above 2.5%, revise below 1.5%
- CPA targets:
  - Reset ($47): target < $20, caution > $28
  - Lab Guide ($27): target < $12, caution > $18
  - Course ($97): target < $35, caution > $50
  - Membership ($27/mo): target < $22 initially
- Frequency: if > 2.5 and CTR drops, rotate creatives

## D) Funnel Simplification Rules
- Keep one clean membership only at $27/month.
- Avoid conflicting offers on first click.
- One ad set -> one product page -> one primary CTA.
- Do not show two primary checkout buttons for different offers above the fold.

Primary CTA tree:
1. Ad -> 21-Day Reset checkout
2. Post-purchase upsell -> Thyroid Health Mastery
3. Non-buyer retargeting -> Lab Guide
4. Buyer follow-up (email + retargeting) -> Wellness Circle
