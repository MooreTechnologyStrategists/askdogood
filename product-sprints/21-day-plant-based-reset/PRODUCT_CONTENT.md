# 21-Day Plant-Based Reset

## Quick Start
- Pick your start date.
- Choose your prep day (default: Sunday).
- Print or duplicate this guide.
- Commit to consistency over perfection.

## Reset Rules (Simple)
1. Build each plate from this order: fiber, protein, healthy fat, hydration.
2. Prep once, eat multiple times.
3. Keep at least 3 emergency fallback meals ready.
4. Track daily wins, not just scale changes.

## Week 1: Stabilize
Goal: reduce decision fatigue and create meal rhythm.

Daily focus:
- Day 1: Kitchen reset + grocery plan
- Day 2: Breakfast consistency
- Day 3: Lunch prep rhythm
- Day 4: Dinner template night
- Day 5: Hydration + snack cleanup
- Day 6: Grocery review + adjust
- Day 7: Reflection + prep for Week 2

## Week 2: Strengthen
Goal: improve energy and digestion support.

Daily focus:
- Day 8: Add one anti-inflammatory staple
- Day 9: Upgrade protein consistency
- Day 10: Improve fiber distribution
- Day 11: Prep low-energy fallback meals
- Day 12: Reduce ultra-processed defaults
- Day 13: Portion and timing check
- Day 14: Reflection + prep for Week 3

## Week 3: Lock In
Goal: make the system sustainable after Day 21.

Daily focus:
- Day 15: Build your permanent grocery template
- Day 16: Build your 10-meal rotation list
- Day 17: Tighten prep workflow to 90 minutes
- Day 18: Simplify shopping route + list
- Day 19: Rehearse a busy-day plan
- Day 20: Rehearse a travel/social plan
- Day 21: Final review + next 30-day plan

## Plate Template
Use this for lunch and dinner:
- 40% non-starchy vegetables
- 25% plant protein
- 25% smart carbs
- 10% healthy fats

## Emergency Fallback Meals
Keep ingredients for these always available:
- Lentil bowl with greens and olive oil
- Overnight oats + chia + berries
- Chickpea salad wrap with avocado

## Daily Tracker
Track each day with Yes/No:
- Planned meals before noon
- Hydration target hit
- At least one protein-centered meal
- One prep action completed
- Energy score (1-10)
- Digestion comfort score (1-10)

## Final Reflection
At the end of Day 21, answer:
- What worked repeatedly?
- What failed repeatedly?
- What 3 habits will continue for the next 30 days?

Consistency is the product. Keep moving.
