How To Pitch This To Parents

  • Lead with the money saver: “Free and low-cost family plans in PG County.”
  • Post the flyer in neighborhood groups with one simple line: “Trying to get the kids out the house? Start here.”
  • Mention the exact age fit when you share it: toddlers, school-age kids, or all ages.
  • Make it easier by suggesting a meetup time: “We’re pulling up at 10:30 if anybody wants to join.”
  • Good places to post: apartment lobbies, libraries, churches, mailboxes, playground boards, and parent group chats.
Best share line: “Family fun in PG that doesn’t cost a fortune.”

Quick Family Plan

  1. Pick one event that fits your crew’s age and energy.
  2. Text one other parent so it feels like a real outing.
  3. Bring water, snacks, and a stroller if needed.
  4. Register early if it’s a paid or small-group event.
