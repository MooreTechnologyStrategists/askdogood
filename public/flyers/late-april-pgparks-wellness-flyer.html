How To Market This So Adults Care

  • Lead with the feeling: “Need a cheap reset before May starts?”
  • Use words like movement, stress relief, community, and low-pressure wellness.
  • Post it where adults already stop: coffee shops, churches, community boards, apartment lobbies, beauty salons, and Facebook groups.
  • For digital sharing, highlight one anchor event: Urban Line Dancing or the Herbs, Tea, and Arts Festival.
  • Add a simple invitation: “I’m going — meet me there?”
Best share line: “Late-April wellness plans in PG County that won’t wreck your wallet.”
