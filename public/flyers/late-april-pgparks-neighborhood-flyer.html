How To Market This So People Actually Show Up

  • Lead with “Free and low-cost local plans” instead of “programs and recreation.”
  • Use lines like “Pick one event, text one friend, and pull up.”
  • Post this near mailboxes, community boards, laundromats, churches, salons, and neighborhood Facebook groups.
  • Highlight one anchor event when sharing digitally: Montpelier Festival of Herbs, Tea, and the Arts.
  • For parents, push the family angle. For adults, push stress relief, movement, and community.
Best digital hook: “Late-April plans in PG County that won’t wreck your wallet.”
