3. **Natural:** Big, styled, voluminous
4. **Straight:** Sleek long hair with edge control laid

- **Arms:** Full or half sleeves visible
- **Style:** Mix of meaningful (flowers, quotes, symbols) and artistic
- **Placement:** Arms, shoulders, possibly chest/collarbone area, back of neck
- **Aesthetic:** Not prison tattoos - professional tattoo art, well-done, colorful or black/grey

- Tailored blazer + designer sneakers
- Fitted hoodie + gold jewelry
- Oversized button-down (styled) + fitted jeans
- Leather jacket + heels
- Streetwear brands mixed with high fashion

**Always include:**
- Gold jewelry (hoops, chains, rings)
- Nails done (coffin or almond shape, neutral or bold)
- Coordinated outfit (intentional, not thrown together)
- Confidence in posture
