# AskDoGood Marketing Autopilot Playbook

## Purpose
This playbook lets one product stay in front of the audience every week with a repeatable system.

## Weekly cadence
1. Monday: homepage/shop CTA check + email #1
2. Tuesday: social post #1 + engagement replies
3. Wednesday: social post #2 + short video #1
4. Thursday: email #2 + social post #3
5. Friday: social post #4 + short video #2
6. Saturday: social post #5 + community interaction
7. Sunday: email #3 + week performance review

## Daily minimum actions
- Publish one high-quality CTA touchpoint.
- Reply to comments/messages for 15-20 minutes.
- Record clicks and sales from each channel.

## Core metrics
- Checkout clicks
- Checkout conversion rate
- Revenue
- Best converting channel

## Decision rule
If conversion rate drops for 3 consecutive days:
- Swap the headline angle
- Reorder CTA placement on homepage/shop
- Test a tighter offer deadline

## Energy-protection rule
If founder bandwidth is low, prioritize this order only:
1. Email
2. Homepage/shop CTA update
3. One social post
