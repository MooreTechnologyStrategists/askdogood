## Implementation in Beehiiv

### Setting Up the Campaign:

1. **Create an Automation in Beehiiv:**
   - Go to Automations â†’ Create New
   - Trigger: "Subscriber joins specific segment" (tag: wordpress-migration)
   - Or: "Subscriber subscribes to list"

2. **Add Email Sequence:**
   - Add each email as a step
   - Set delays between emails (Day 0, +3 days, +7 days, etc.)
   - Enable click tracking and analytics

3. **Tag Your Imported Subscribers:**
   - When importing CSV, add tag: "wordpress-migration"
   - This triggers the automation for all 1500 subscribers

4. **Customize with Merge Tags:**
   - `{{first_name}}` - subscriber's first name
   - `{{email}}` - subscriber's email
   - Use fallbacks: `{{first_name | default: "friend"}}`

5. **Track Performance:**
   - Monitor open rates (aim for 20-30%)
   - Track click-through rates (aim for 3-5%)
   - Adjust subject lines based on performance

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Personalization:** Always use first names when available
2. **Mobile-First:** Over 60% will read on mobile - keep it concise
3. **Clear CTAs:** One primary button per email
4. **A/B Test:** Test subject lines on 20% of list, send winner to remaining 80%
5. **Send Time:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9am-11am EST typically perform best
6. **Re-engagement:** If someone doesn't engage after 6 emails, add them to a "dormant" segment and try a different approach in 30 days
