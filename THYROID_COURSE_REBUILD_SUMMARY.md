## HOW TO CREATE YOUR VIDEOS

### OPTION 1: InVideo AI (RECOMMENDED - Easiest)
1. Go to **invideo.ai**
2. Click "Create with AI"
3. Paste each video script
4. Add prompt: "Create a professional health education video with stock footage, text overlays, and female AI voiceover. Keep it engaging and informative."
5. AI generates full video in 5 minutes
6. Edit colors, music, transitions if desired
7. Download as MP4
8. **Time per video:** 5-10 minutes AI creation + 5 min editing = 15 min total

**Cost:** $25/month subscription (creates unlimited videos)

### OPTION 2: Pictory.ai
1. Go to **pictory.ai**
2. Click "Script to Video"
3. Paste script
4. Choose professional template
5. AI auto-generates scenes
6. Select voiceover style
7. Download video

**Cost:** $19-99/month

### OPTION 3: Canva Video (FREE)
1. Open **Canva** → Create "Video"
2. Choose educational template
3. Copy script into text boxes
4. Add animations & transitions
5. Record voiceover OR use text-to-speech
6. Download as MP4

**Cost:** FREE (or $13/month for Pro)

### OPTION 4: PowerPoint + Screen Recording
1. Create slides with key teaching points
2. Add animations & visuals
3. Record narration (click: Slideshow → Record Slideshow)
4. Export as video
5. OR use Loom to screen record

**Cost:** FREE (if have PowerPoint)
