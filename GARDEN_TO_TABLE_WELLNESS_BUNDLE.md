## üåª PART 3: COMPANION PLANTING GUIDE

### **What is Companion Planting?**
Growing complementary plants together to:
- Repel pests naturally
- Attract beneficial insects
- Improve soil health
- Maximize space

### **Best Companion Plant Combinations:**

**Tomatoes + Basil**
- Basil repels aphids, whiteflies, tomato hornworms
- Improves tomato flavor
- Both love full sun

**Carrots + Rosemary**
- Rosemary repels carrot flies
- Carrots loosen soil for rosemary roots

**Lettuce + Chives**
- Chives repel aphids
- Lettuce benefits from light shade

**Beans + Savory**
- Savory repels bean beetles
- Beans fix nitrogen in soil

**Cucumbers + Dill**
- Dill attracts beneficial wasps
- Repels cucumber beetles

**Plants to AVOID Planting Together:**
- Tomatoes + Brassicas (kale, broccoli) - compete for nutrients
- Fennel + Most plants (inhibits growth)
- Onions + Beans (stunts bean growth)

## ‚úÖ TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE

### **Problem: Yellowing Leaves**
- **Cause:** Overwatering or nitrogen deficiency
- **Solution:** Let soil dry more between waterings, add compost

### **Problem: Herbs Bolting (Flowering Too Soon)**
- **Cause:** Heat stress
- **Solution:** Plant in cooler weather, provide afternoon shade, harvest regularly

### **Problem: Leggy Plants (Tall, Weak Stems)**
- **Cause:** Not enough light
- **Solution:** Move to sunnier spot or add grow light

### **Problem: Root Rot**
- **Cause:** Overwatering, poor drainage
- **Solution:** Ensure drainage holes, let soil dry between waterings

### **Problem: Pests**
- **Solution:** See Natural Pest Control section above
