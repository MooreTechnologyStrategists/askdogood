## 6-Week Reset Program
Working title:
AskDoGood 6-Week Reset

### Week 1: Awareness And Detox
- Identify the habits, foods, and stress loops that are draining energy
- Reduce sugar, ultra-processed foods, and obvious inflammatory triggers
- Introduce hydration, basic smoothies, and a simple reset rhythm
- Outcome: I can finally see what has been hurting me

### Week 2: Nutrition Reset
- Anti-inflammatory eating basics
- Smoothie systems and healing-food routines
- Grocery strategy for real budgets and real schedules
- Outcome: I know what to eat now

### Week 3: Movement And Energy
- Walking routines
- Beginner workouts and circulation support
- Gentle energy restoration without burnout
- Outcome: I can feel my energy coming back

### Week 4: Mental Reset
- Stress reduction
- Journaling and prayer or reflection
- Identifying emotional triggers and habit disruptions
- Outcome: I feel more in control

### Week 5: Income Activation
- Intro to tech pathways, cloud, and AI awareness
- Side hustle ideas and practical next steps
- Resume, confidence, and opportunity mindset
- Outcome: I can see a path forward financially

### Week 6: Lifestyle Integration
- Build routines that can survive real life
- Long-term habits and home systems
- Family and household alignment
- Outcome: This can become my new normal
